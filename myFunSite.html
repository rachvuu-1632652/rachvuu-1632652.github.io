Guarantees:

The Olympic Pavilion requires notification of the exact number of guests within four (4) business days prior to your event. Once received, this number constitutes a guarantee. If fewer than the guaranteed number of guests attend the event, the patron is charged for the original guaranteed number. If no written or verbal guarantee is received, the contracted number will be considered your guarantee. A charge of $100 will apply should the attendance be lower than this minimum.
